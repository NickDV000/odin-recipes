Caprese Stuffed Avocado

Description

Caprese Stuffed Avocado is a delightful twist on the classic Caprese salad, combining the creamy richness of ripe avocados with the fresh, vibrant flavors of tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil. Each halved avocado is generously filled with diced tomatoes, soft mozzarella balls, and aromatic basil leaves, all drizzled with a tangy balsamic glaze and a splash of extra virgin olive oil. This dish is a perfect blend of creamy, tangy, and savory, offering a refreshing, low-carb option that's both visually appealing and satisfying. Ideal as a light lunch, appetizer, or side dish, Caprese Stuffed Avocado is a delicious and healthy choice for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and cut in half
  • 1/4 cup fresh mozzarella pearls
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 6 fresh basil leaves, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Arrange avocado halves onto a plate.
  2. Toss together mozzarella pearls, cherry tomatoes, and basil in a small bowl. Spoon the Caprese filling into each avocado half and drizzle with balsamic vinegar. Season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.
